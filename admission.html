Admissions Process

Learn how to apply for your child's enrollment at God's Angels Junior School

How to Apply

At God's Angels Junior School, we welcome families who share our commitment to Christ-centered education. Our admissions process is designed to be straightforward and supportive.

1

Initial Inquiry

Contact our admissions office to schedule a tour of our campus and learn more about our programs.

2

Application Submission

Complete and submit the application form along with required documents (birth certificate, previous school records, immunization records).

3

Interview & Assessment

Participate in an informal interview with your child and parents to understand your family's needs and expectations.

4

Decision Notification

You will receive notification of the admissions decision within 2 weeks of your interview.

5

Enrollment Confirmation

Upon acceptance, submit enrollment forms and required fees to secure your child's spot.

Required Documents

  • Completed application form
  • Birth certificate (certified copy)
  • Previous school records/transcripts
  • Immunization records
  • Two recent photographs of the student
  • Proof of residence
