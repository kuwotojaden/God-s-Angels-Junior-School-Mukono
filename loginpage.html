    Admission Information

    Important details about our admission process

    Admission Requirements

    For admission to God's Angels Junior School, please ensure you have the following documents ready:

    • Birth certificate or passport
    • Previous school records (if applicable)
    • Immunization records
    • Two recent passport photos

    Admission Process

    Our admission process includes:

    1. Application submission (completed online)
    2. Review of application by our admissions committee
    3. Notification of acceptance
    4. Payment of registration fee
    5. Orientation session for parents and students

    Important Information

    Application Deadline

    Applications for the next academic year are accepted until December 31st.

    Registration Fee

    The registration fee is $50 and is non-refundable upon acceptance.

    Required Documents

    Please have all required documents ready before submitting your application.
